 Is AI replacing Actors, too?
Site Author
 Todd Spangler
Abstract


 The article calls into question if AI are going to fully take over and replace actors all together. It calls back to the Writer's Strike but delves more into the actors' stance on the matter since a few notable actors also joined in on the strike. It also goes over the fears as many filmmakers, screenwriters, and actors have towards the growing advancements of AI. It is evolving at such a fast rate and it's becoming more smarter that is could easily and flawlessly "replicate" an actor's likeness on screen. So, the fears of how efficient AI is is warranted.
